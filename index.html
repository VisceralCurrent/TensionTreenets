Our Vision

Crafting Spaces Above the Earth.

Tension Treenets was born from a passion for high-altitude adventure and a deep respect for the forest canopy. Inspired by the rugged beauty of mountain landscapes—where the orange rock meets the white snow—we design nets that aren't just structures, but living art.

Whether it's a "Sky Couch" for a backyard retreat or a multi-story "Crow's Nest" for a resort, our tensioned systems offer a unique, exhilarating way to experience the world from above.

"The forest is our canvas; tension is our brush."

What We Build

Custom Treenets

Permanently hand-woven platforms installed between living anchors. Ideal for relaxation and meditation.

Adventure Nets

High-tension systems designed for movement, play, and vertical exploration. Rigged for safety and speed.

Safe Rigging

Professional inspection and tension maintenance using climbing-grade static ropes and paracord.

Designed for Trees, Not Just People.

We believe in "doing dangerous things carefully." Our installation process utilizes custom wood blocks and padded anchors to prevent girdling and ensure the trees continue to sway naturally in the wind. We protect the forest integrity that supports us.
